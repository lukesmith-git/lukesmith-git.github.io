Projects:

The following are current projects I've finished and a short description.

Indeed:

I scraped Indeed using scrapy and scrape-ops to get information for Data Science and Data Analyst jobs. I looked at salaries, age of postings, and the programming languages required.

Predicting Bike Ownership:

This was for my Data Science Certification. I was given data from reviews (rated 1-5) of several models of bikes and whether the reviewer actually owned the bike. I used this information to build a supervised learning model to predict whether a reviewer owned a bike based on the scores given on the review.

Food Poisoning Claims:

This was for my Data Analyst Certification. I was given a dataset regarding food poisoning claims in four regions of Brazil, the amount and length of the claim, how much was paid, etc. I was tasked with cleaning the data, dealing with the missing values, and visualizing and reporting on the data grouped by location.

Networking Analysis:

This was a DataCamp competition. I was given a dataset regarding communications sent and received by employees of a certain company, and a dataset relating the employees department id to the department name. I was tasked with finding the most and least active employees/departments, and recommending which departments the company should focus on to improve overall communication.
