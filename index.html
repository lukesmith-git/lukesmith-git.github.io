Introduction:

Hello! My name is Luke, and I am showcasing projects as I complete them here to fill out my portfolio. My goal, ultimately, is to use this to gain employment in Data Science.

Education:

Originally, I started college as a Psych major at SFASU in 2013. However, I soon realized chemistry was much more interesting, and so I studied chemistry until around 2015-2016, when I dropped out to focus on getting ready to be a father, which was a daunting task at 19 years old. Eventually, I was able to return to school (few years and +2 kids later), and I graduated Magna Cum Laude with my Bachelor's in mathematics from UT Tyler in 2019. During this time, I started learning Python (~2018) and fell in love with using it for mathematical purposes. The following year, I started grad school. While here I took a couple elected courses: Numerical Analysis and Numerical Linear Algebra, in which MatLab was used (forcibly) to look at algorithms including Newton's method, polynomial interpolation, and the fast fourier transform. At the time of creating this, I have completed the necessary requirements to acheive a Master's degree in Mathematics from UT Dallas: 36 hours of courses, all required courses taken, and a GPA above 3.0 in each semester. Unfortunately, the semester I was going to graduate (Fall 2022), I had to have surgery and the school seemed to lose interest in remaining in contact. So while I work that out, I do have official transcripts (and the medical records) to corroborate everything here.
